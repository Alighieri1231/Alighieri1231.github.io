Emilio José Ochoa Alva

Biomedical Engineering PhD student — University of Rochester · Rochester, NY, USA
Email: eochoaal@ur.rochester.edu · Google Scholar · Download CV

I work at the intersection of AI and medical ultrasound, with projects that enable diagnostic imaging in low-resource settings (e.g., obstetrics and breast imaging).

I am advised by PhD. Benjamin Castañeda - Part of the Global Health & Medical Devices Lab.

Research Interests

  • Artificial intelligence for biomedical imaging
  • Ultrasound (VSI, H-scan,elastography, segmentation & detection)
  • Accessible diagnostics for low-resource settings
